The Problem
Image Theft Is Easy

Browsers automatically recognize image files. This means in most cases, if you can see an image on a web page, your computer already downloaded it!

When you "right click" and hit the download button, your computer is actually just making another copy of that image on disk.

Very convenient, we think too convenient...

This is why it is SO EASY to download and steal images online.

But what about watermarks?

Watermarks provide the bare minimum in security, at the cost of the quality of your image. Watermarked images are often resold by thieves online who strip your watermarks, add their own, and take credit for YOUR work. We believe YOUR IMAGES DESERVE BETTER!

Pixel Protection defends not only your property, but your right to showcase it in it's unaltered form. When you secure an image with Pixel Protection you are not sacrificing quality for the illusion of security. You are offering your image to the world as you meant it to be seen; without fear of someone taking it and using it without your permission.

Watermarks, and similar tricks are just that, TRICKS. Thieves who make a living stealing images online have seen all of these. If they haven't, a quick Google search is an easy way to achieve enlightenment. Don't try to trick them, eventually they figure it out.

At Pixel Protection we study the process and science of how browsers work to create a seamless solution, not watermarks or cheap tricks.

Help us spread our patent pending technology to those looking to protect their creativity online.
